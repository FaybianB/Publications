## *Question #14 (Easy): How much is 1 wei of Ether?*

**Answer:** 1 wei is one quintillionth (10^-18) of an Ether.

## Demonstration:

```
Ether denominations:

    - 1 Wei = 10^-18 Ether
    - 1 Gwei = 10^-9 Ether
    - 1 Szabo = 10^-6 Ether
    - 1 Finney = 10^-3 Ether
```

## Further Discussion:

1 wei is the smallest unit of Ether.
