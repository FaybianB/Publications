## *Question #13 (Easy): How much is 1 gwei of Ether?*

**Answer:** 1 Gwei is one billionth (10^-9) of an Ether. 1 Gwei is 10^9 Wei.

## Demonstration:

```
Ether denominations:

    - 1 Wei = 10^-18 Ether
    - 1 Gwei = 10^-9 Ether
    - 1 Szabo = 10^-6 Ether
    - 1 Finney = 10^-3 Ether
```

## Further Discussion:

Gwei is often used to measure gas prices on the Ethereum network.
